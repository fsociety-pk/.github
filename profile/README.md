## Who We Are

FSOCIETY.PK is a focused cybersecurity organization built by our internal team to maintain daily progress, practical skill development, and disciplined documentation.

We are team-led, but public contributors are welcome to join the mission.

## Core Output

- Daily writeups from labs, investigations, and practice sessions
- CTF writeups with step-by-step attack paths and lessons learned
- CVE analysis, exploitation notes, and responsible technical documentation
- Defensive writeups including detection thinking and remediation ideas
- Tooling, scripts, and repeatable workflows for security tasks

## Public Can Join

Anyone serious about cybersecurity can collaborate with us.

1. Open an issue and introduce your interests.
2. Submit a pull request with a writeup, fix, or improvement.
3. Participate in discussions and review work constructively.

Standard: clear writing, reproducible steps, and consistent contribution quality.

## Recommended Writeup Format

- Scope and objective
- Recon and analysis method
- Exploitation or solution path
- Evidence (logs, screenshots, payload notes)
- Mitigation or defensive insight
