## 🧠 Who We Are
We are a college-based **cybersecurity team** on a mission to democratize infosec knowledge.  
As the **team lead**, I drive the vision — alongside **two core members** — to create a structured, daily-learning ecosystem that breaks down vulnerabilities, tools, CTFs, and real-world attacks into **detailed writeups**.

<hr />

## 📁 What We Push (Daily)
- 🔐 **Penetration Testing** — walkthroughs (HTB, TryHackMe, VulnHub)  
- 🛡️ **Blue Team** — analysis & detection engineering  
- 📜 **CVE deep dives** & exploit writeups  
- 🧠 **Custom tooling** & automation scripts  
- 📚 **Study notes** — from networking to binary exploitation

## 🌍 Join The Order
We welcome **anyone** who shares the hunger for learning and contributing.

### ✅ How to join:
1. Open an **issue** in any public repo introducing yourself & your interest  
2. Start small — review a writeup, suggest fixes, or submit your own writeup as a PR  
3. Join our discussions and help us grow

> **📌 Note:** We are beginner-friendly but demand discipline. Every member must push at least **1 writeup per week** (learning log style).
