    ### 🧠 Who We Are
    We are a college-based **cybersecurity team** on a mission to democratize infosec knowledge.  
    As the **team lead**, I drive the vision — alongside **two core members** — to create a structured, daily-learning ecosystem.  
    Every day, we break down vulnerabilities, tools, CTFs, and real-world attacks into **detailed writeups**.
    
    <br/>
    
    ### 📁 What We Push (Daily)
    - 🔐 **Penetration Testing** walkthroughs (HTB, TryHackMe, VulnHub)  
    - 🛡️ **Blue Team** analysis & detection engineering  
    - 📜 **CVE deep dives** & exploit writeups  
    - 🧠 **Custom tooling** & automation scripts  
    - 📚 **Study notes** — from networking to binary exploitation  
    
    <br/>

    <br/>
    
    ### 🌍 Join The Order
    We welcome **anyone** who shares the hunger for learning and contributing.
    
    #### ✅ How to join:
    1. Read our [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](https://github.com/fsociety-pk/.github/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) (coming soon)  
    2. Open an **issue** in any public repo introducing yourself & your interest  
    3. Start small — review a writeup, suggest fixes, or submit your own writeup as a PR  
    
    > [!NOTE]
    > We are beginner-friendly but demand discipline. Every member must push at least **1 writeup per week** (learning log style).
